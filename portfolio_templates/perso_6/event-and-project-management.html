Peaks Challenge 2017

During Bicycle Networks busiest event period I came on board to help them deliver Peaks Challenge 2017. On Sunday 5 November 2017 over 300 riders tackled the final 250km Peaks Challenge Cradle Mountain Tasmania ride with the hope of becoming Peaks Challenge finishers.

The logistics that come with such an event are demanding, but it was the perfect day and an extremely successful event!
