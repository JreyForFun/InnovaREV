Frequently Asked Questions

Do you do custom designs?

Yes! We specialize in custom furniture and cabinetry. We can work from your sketches or photos, or design something completely new for you.

What types of wood do you use?

We work with a wide variety of domestic and exotic hardwoods, including Oak, Walnut, Cherry, Maple, and Mahogany. We prioritize sustainably sourced lumber.

How long does a project take?

Timelines vary depending on the complexity of the project. A custom table might take 4-6 weeks, while a full kitchen could take 8-12 weeks.

Do you offer delivery?

Yes, we offer white-glove delivery and installation services for all our local clients. Long-distance shipping can also be arranged.
